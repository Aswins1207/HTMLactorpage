VIJAY THALAPATHY

Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is an Indian actor and playback singer who works predominantly in Tamil cinema12. He was born on June 22, 1974 in Chennai, India13. He is referred to by fans and media as "Thalapathy" (commander) and is the highest paid actor in Tamil cinema2. He excels in acting, singing and dancing and has a fan following in China, Japan, UK, and France3.
